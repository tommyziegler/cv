About

Hello! I am Tommy Ziegler. Cloud, TDD, RESTful, BI/BigData, Agile and Spring enthusiast - Love to be in Riga

I have many years of experience in creating individual enterprise and web applications. My field of activity includes the requirements survey, the process, integration and software architecture, the technical design as well as the implementation of complex enterprise solutions.

I have developed my particular strengths through numerous projects in the field of Java EE technologies and Java EE based frameworks from the open source environment. My main areas of expertise are microservice landscapes, Amazon Web Services, agile development methods, data storage technologies, and digital rights management. In my projects, I took various roles and worked as a developer, team leader, Scrum coach and technical contact. In addition to the project work, I deal with current trends in computer science.
Since 2006, I regularly attend developer conferences such as the Chaos Communication Congress, the Devoxx, but also local meetup groups for sharing experiences with like-minded people.

I like to share my experiences and my knowledge with my customers in order to make their projects successful together.
